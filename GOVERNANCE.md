# Governance

Mojibake is a maintainer-led open source project. Maintainers set the project direction, review
contributions, manage releases, and have final responsibility for technical and community
decisions.

Contributors participate by opening issues, proposing changes, reviewing PRs, and helping other
users. Decisions should be made openly in the relevant issue or pull request, with rough consensus
preferred. When consensus cannot be reached, the maintainers make the final decision based on the
project's goals: correctness, portability, performance, and a small, self-contained implementation.

Regular contributors may be invited to become maintainers based on sustained, constructive
participation and sound technical judgment. Maintainers who step down should, when possible, help
transfer their responsibilities to an active contributor.

Changes to this governance model follow the normal pull request process described in
[CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).
